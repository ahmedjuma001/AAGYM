Weekly Progression Tips

  • Week 1: Light weights, learn proper form
  • Week 2: Increase weights slightly
  • Week 3: Add more sets or reduce rest
  • Week 4: Maximize weights and performance

General Fitness Tips

  • Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily
  • Get 7–9 hours of sleep
  • Use whey protein if needed after workouts
  • Track progress with weekly photos or logs
  • Focus on long-term consistency
